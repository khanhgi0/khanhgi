BLOG GIOI THIEU VE DU LICHH

Viet Nam so huu vo van canh dep va thien nhien hung trang, la va doc dao, hay diem qua 10 thang canh noi tieng duoc du khach nuoc ngoai binh chon nhe.


Giang noi bat voi ve dep vua hung vi, gai goc lai vua tho mong den nao long. Noi day noi tieng voi Cao nguyen da Dong Van (Cong vien dia chat toan cau) va dong song Nho Que xanh biec chay duoi chan deo Ma Pi Leng huyen thoai.

Trai nghiem: Di thuyen tren song Nho Que qua Hem Tu San - hem vuc sau nhat Dong Nam A, check-in cot co Lung Cu, Dinh thu ho Vuong.

Thoi diem vang: Thang 10 - 12 la mua hoa Tam Giac Mach no ro va mua hoa Cai vang, bien cao nguyen da thanh buc tranh ruc ro.

Nam o do cao 1.600m, Sapa so huu nhung ky quan thien nhien dang cap the gioi. Dien hinh phai ke den Thung lung Muong Hoa voi nhung thua ruong bac thang tung duoc tap chi Travel & Leisure binh chon vao top dep nhat the gioi.

Trai nghiem phai thu: Chinh phuc dinh Fansipan bang cap treo 3 day hien dai va san bien may bong benh, trekking ban Cat Cat.

Mua dep nhat: Thang 9 (mua lua chin vang) hoac thang 12 - 1 (co hoi ngam bang tuyet hiem co).
